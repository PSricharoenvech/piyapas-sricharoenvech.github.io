Research

My research focuses on environmental soil chemistry, with particular interest in the fate and transformation of metals and nutrients in soils impacted by sea-level rise.

Methods commonly used: Routine chemical extractions, synchrotron-based X-ray absorption spectroscopy (XAS).

Ongoing Research

  • Effects of Salinity from Saltwater Intrusion on the Availability, Speciation, and Release of Phosphorus in Soils in Durian Orchards in Nonthaburi Province, Thailand
    Use several chemical extractions and synchrotron-based X-ray absorption spectroscopy (XAS) to investigate how a increase in salinity impacts soil phosphorus speciation and release from agricultural soils.
  • Potential of Biochar for Removing Microplastics from Agricultural Runoff
    Evaluate the potential of biochar as a low-cost filter for microplastics in agricultural runoff water.
