Piyapas Sricharoenvech

Lecturer, Kasetsart University, Bangkok, Thailand

About

Piyapas Sricharoenvech is a Lecturer in the Department of Soil Science at Kasetsart University, Bangkok, Thailand. Her research lies in the field of environmental soil chemistry, with a focus on understanding the fate, transformation, and availability of metals and nutrients in soils impacted by sea-level rise and salinity intrusion.

Her work combines conventional chemical extraction techniques with advanced synchrotron-based X-ray absorption spectroscopy to investigate soil chemical processes at both bulk and molecular scales.

Research Interests

  • Environmental soil chemistry
  • Phosphorus and nutrient cycling
  • Impacts of sea level rise on soil chemical processes
